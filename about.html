Woodworks Design
Built to order. Built to endure. Built to inspire.


Our manufacturing facility is located in Middlefield, Ohio. Our fine furniture and cabinetry can be shipped anywhere in the USA.

We pride ourselves on being a noteworthy resource for the design community as well as working directly with homeowners to develop the perfect solution for any project.

We Woodworks Design are commited to quality custom craftsmanship. Let us help make your dreams a reality.
